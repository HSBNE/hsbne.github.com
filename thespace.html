221C MacArthur Ave, Eagle Farm, QLD 4009

Our campus includes two buildings with an enclosed courtyard on a 3,500m2 plot. We are located in The Factories, which is right next to Eat St. Google Maps finds us best but here are some suggestions: We recommend you book in for a tour on a tuesday open night.

How can I get to HSBNE?

By Car...

Ideal Driving Route (from the CBD)

  1. Make your way onto the ICB Northbound, follow through onto Kingsford Smith Drive.
  2. Take a right at Remora Rd, and a left at the traffic lights, onto MacArthur Ave.
  3. Head east for roughly 900m and turn right into our driveway (look for big trees in the middle of the road).

We share our driveway with one of the car parks for Eat Street Markets. Look for a stack of shipping containers on our corner displaying a solar powered car.

We have on-site parking off the MacArthur Ave driveway plus additional parking adjacent to Eat Street's car park on the river side of HSBNE.

By Public Transport...

  • Translink Planner
  • Walking Route from Northshore Hamilton Ferry Terminal
  • Walking Route from Racecourse Rd, Hamilton Stop 20 (300 bus)
  • Walking Route from Lancaster Rd, Ascot Stop 25/15 (301 bus)

The nearest CityCat terminal is the Northshore Hamilton CityCat Terminal, around 800m away. Once you arrive, walk up the ramp and turn left. Follow the river past tennis courts and past Eat Street. As you cross the car park past Eat Street, HSBNE will be on your right as your approach the road.

The nearest bus stop is Theodore St at MacArthur Av, Hamilton (stops 200308 and 200309) serviced by the 302, 304 and 305 busses.

By Riding...

Shortest most direct route, 8km

This is the shortest quickest way there from the city with a bit of traffic, pedestrians and a mix of bike lanes and main roads. Mostly flat terrain and if you're comfortable riding on main roads is the most efficient route.

The scenic route, 15km

This route follows the Brisbane River for most of the trip. Don't forget your sunscreen and water bottle!
