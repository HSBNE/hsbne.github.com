Welcome!

HSBNE, the HackerSpace Brisbane, is a community workshop located in Sumner

We're a group of people brought together by a shared interest in tinkering, creating, and making things. HSBNE is entirely run by its members — we have no employees or staff. Everything that happens here is thanks to the shared effort of our community.

Our space currently includes:

  • Woodworking
  • Digital Fabrication
  • Metalworking
  • Electronics
  • Crafts

Open Days

Visit during the day and explore what we do

Every Saturday from 10 am – 2 pm, our doors are open to the public. Come for a tour at 11 am, stay for a chat, and enjoy pizza around 12:30 pm. It’s the perfect time to see the space in action and meet our members.

Book a tour

Open Nights

Drop in after hours and see the space come alive

Every Tuesday evening, HSBNE opens its doors for the public to explore, chat, and create. Members start arriving from 5 pm, and a guided tour of the workshop begins at 6:30 pm. Whether you’re curious or already building something — come hang out!

Talk with us

Say hello, ask a question, or just hang out online

Want to ask a few questions about the space? Or just chat with someone from the community? The best way to do that is by joining our Discord server. Discord is a free chat app where you can connect with like-minded people and get to know us better. Click the link below to join our server!
