The tools

These are some of the key tools available at Craftpunk to help members with their craft projects.

Sublimation Printer

Sublimation printing is a digital printing technique that uses heat to transfer dye directly into a material. It results in high-quality, durable prints for fabrics, ceramics, and other materials.

Vacuum Chamber

A vacuum chamber is used to remove air and gases, ensuring bubble-free castings for resin and other materials, often used in crafting to improve the finish.

Vinyl Cutter

A vinyl cutter is a computer-controlled machine that cuts intricate designs and shapes out of vinyl sheets, perfect for creating decals, signs, and stencils.

Badge Maker

A badge maker presses printed designs into custom badges or buttons. Perfect for creating event memorabilia or personal accessories.

Embroidery Machine

Computer-controlled embroidery machines that stitch detailed designs into fabric, ideal for textile art and custom garment creation.

Overlocker

Used for sewing, trimming, and finishing fabric edges to prevent fraying and provide a professional finish, especially for stretch and knit fabrics.

Glass Grinder

A glass grinder smooths edges and reshapes glass pieces, particularly useful for stained glass crafting and other detailed glasswork projects.
