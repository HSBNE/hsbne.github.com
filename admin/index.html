As a member run club, here you can find all the boring but necessary documentation. Meeting agendas, notes, mandates, policy are all here.

Group Rules

The HSBNE bible, as it were. Our group rules are a fairly well modified version of the Model Rules for Incorporation. When in doubt, refer to these. Any member is welcome to raise a change to these at a meeting, however it costs HSBNE a nominal fee to re-file.

Code of Conduct

The code of conduct is a set of guidelines that outline an acceptable standard of behaviour and conduct of all HSBNE members whether it be online, in a pub or at HSBNE.

Policies

We have a number of non official but enforced guidelines within the group. These are typically the result of a meeting.

Dispute Process

Disagreements between members are inevitable. Through experience, HSBNE has provided this dispute resolution policy.

Ongoing Costs

The Executive tries to keep any ongoing cost commitments by HSBNE documented here.

Executive History

The history of members who have dedicated their time to making HSBNE run.

Meetings

Agendas and their corresponding minutes of past HSBNE meetings.
