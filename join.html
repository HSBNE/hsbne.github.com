Come along and meet us! We have no requirement for membership. We ask that you join when you want to contribute back to HSBNE.

Ready to Join? Follow these steps:

  1. Book in for a tour

    Get a feel for us in person before joining to make sure the culture will work for you. As part of contact-tracing, you need to book in for a tour.

    The executive is also responsible for vetting your membership, so we like to get to know you!

    1. Book in for a Tour

  2. Register an account on our member portal

    Create an account in our membership portal. You'll need this account to sign up for an induction.

    2. Register Account

  3. Get Inducted

    Once you've signed up to our membership portal, you will receive an email from us to book in for an induction. Inductions are held regularly, mostly on Tuesdays at 7pm.

    Please bring photo ID. If you chose the starving hacker membership level, you'll also need proof of your eligibility.

  4. Send payment to HSBNE

    After you complete your induction and we've accepted your membership, you will receive an invoice. This may take a few days.

  5. Get site access

    After your payment has cleared we'll activate the card you received during your induction. You will be notified by email when this happens.

Membership at HSBNE

Benefits

  • A warm fuzzy feeling.
  • 24hr Access to the Port Hack Campus via RFID
  • Voting rights in the group
  • One 55L storage tub in member storage (If required)
  • Discounted Classes

Fee Structure

Backer Hacker
  • $100 per month, no further discounts.
Standard Hacker
  • $60 per month, or
  • $55 per month, when paying 3 months in advance
  • 1 Month $60
  • 2 Months $120
  • 3 Months $165
  • 6 Months $330
  • 12 Months $660
Starving Hacker
  • $30 per month, no further discounts

    • To be eligible for this level you must be genuinely unable to afford the standard tier. Being a student or unemployed generally makes you eligible. You will require proof of eligibility during your induction.
Lifetime Hacker
  • $0 per month, for life

Available to any member having paid 10 years membership either normally through the above options, or in advance. Paying in advance takes into account prior membership, and is calculated at:

( 10years x 12months - <Months of Accrued Membership> ) x $55
