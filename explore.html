HSBNE, is a community-driven makerspace located in Brisbane, Australia, where individuals with diverse interests can come together to collaborate, learn, and work on various projects encompassing areas such as woodworking, metalworking, blacksmithing, electronics, 3D printing, and more.

Check out some of our tools that we have in your area of interest below. This is not a list of all our available tools, so its always best to visit us in person.

  • Woodwork
  • Digifab
  • Craftpunk
