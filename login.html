HSBNE's Wiki

HSBNE's collection of member maintained documentation. All detailed information on the facilities and services available to members are documented here.

HSBNE's Portal

Members' RF cards allow access to vending machines, doors, machines and tools. HSBNE's portal is the system that coordinates all of those services.

HSBNE's Discord Server

Continue your conversations with other members even when you're not in the space. There's always a conversation to join or start yourself here.

HSBNE's Discourse Forum

Some discussions need some time to breathe, and Discord moves a bit too quickly for those kinds of discussions. Forum posts can wait until you're ready to participate.
