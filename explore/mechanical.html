--- title: Mechanical layout: default ---

The mechanical area has tools for building and reparing cars, lawnmowers, pushbikes, etc.

Service Ramps

Car ramps are inclined structures that allow vehicles to drive onto them, providing easier access to the underside of the vehicle for maintenance and repairs.

Engine Crane

An engine crane, also called an engine hoist or cherry picker, is a device used to lift and move heavy engines or components with ease and precision.

Car Jacks

A car jack is a mechanical device used to lift vehicles off the ground for maintenance, tire changes, or repairs.

Creeper

A creeper is a low-profile platform on wheels that allows mechanics to comfortably slide under vehicles for maintenance and repairs.

Jack Stands

Jack stands are supportive devices used to hold vehicles securely in an elevated position during maintenance and repairs, providing stability and safety.

Floor Jack

A floor jack is a hydraulic tool used to lift vehicles off the ground for maintenance, repairs, or tire changes.
